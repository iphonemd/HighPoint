Sealants

Our professional sealant services provide superior protection for buildings of all types, preventing water infiltration and air leaks that can damage your structure. We use only the highest quality materials to ensure long-lasting durability and performance.

Benefits Include:

  • Enhanced building envelope protection
  • Prevention of water and air infiltration
  • Improved energy efficiency
  • Extended building lifespan
  • Prevention of mold and mildew growth

Applications:

Our sealant services are ideal for expansion joints, control joints, window perimeters, door frames, precast concrete panels, EIFS systems, and much more.

  • Plaza deck and podium waterproofing

Site & Paving Sealants

Our site and paving sealant services help extend the lifespan of your pavement while maintaining a safe and professional appearance. Proper sealant application prevents water infiltration that can cause damage to your pavement infrastructure.

Applications Include:

  • Parking lot joint sealing
  • Sidewalk expansion joints
  • Concrete pavement joints
  • Asphalt crack sealing
  • Control joint sealing

Benefits:

Our site and paving sealants protect against water damage, freeze-thaw cycles, and UV degradation, while enhancing the visual appeal of your property and preventing weed growth and pest infiltration in pavement cracks.

Below Grade Waterproofing

Our below grade waterproofing solutions are designed to protect foundations, basements, and underground structures from water infiltration. We apply durable, high-performance waterproofing systems designed to keep your building dry and secure for years to come.

Our Below Grade Waterproofing Includes:

  • Foundation wall waterproofing
  • Blind-side waterproofing
  • Sheet membrane waterproofing
  • Bentonite clay systems
  • Fluid-applied waterproofing

Why It's Important:

Effective below grade waterproofing is essential for preventing structural damage, mold growth, and interior water damage. Our systems are designed to withstand hydrostatic pressure and provide long-term protection for your building's foundation.

Air Barrier

By applying high-quality air barrier systems, we help improve indoor air quality, reduce energy costs, and protect your structure from moisture infiltration and environmental wear. Our air barrier solutions create a continuous seal around your building envelope.

Benefits of Our Air Barrier Systems:

  • Enhanced energy efficiency
  • Reduced heating and cooling costs
  • Prevention of condensation within walls
  • Improved indoor air quality
  • Reduced risk of mold and mildew

Our Air Barrier Solutions:

We offer a variety of air barrier solutions, including fluid-applied membranes, sheet membranes, and spray-applied foams, all designed to create an effective continuous air barrier that meets building code requirements and enhances building performance.

Traffic Coating

Our traffic coating systems provide superior protection for parking structures, garages, walkways, and other high-traffic areas exposed to vehicular and pedestrian traffic. These specialized coatings create a durable, waterproof membrane that protects your concrete surfaces from deterioration caused by water penetration, de-icing salts, UV exposure, and general wear and tear.

Benefits Include:

  • Extended service life of parking structures and decks
  • Protection against water intrusion and concrete deterioration
  • Resistance to oil, gas, and chemical spills
  • Improved skid resistance for safety
  • Customizable appearance with color options
  • Reduced maintenance costs and downtime

Applications:

Our traffic coating services are ideal for parking garages, stadium concourses, mechanical rooms, plaza decks, balconies, and any concrete surface exposed to vehicular or pedestrian traffic. We offer multiple coating options with varying thicknesses and durability levels to meet your specific needs and budget.

Maintenance Services

Our regular maintenance services ensure that your caulking and waterproofing systems remain effective and provide long-lasting protection. Proactive maintenance helps identify potential issues before they lead to costly repairs.

Our Maintenance Services Include:

  • Regular inspections of sealant and waterproofing systems
  • Identification and repair of failing sealants
  • Cleaning and preparation of joints for resealing
  • Application of new sealants where needed
  • Documentation and reporting on system condition

Benefits of Regular Maintenance:

Regular maintenance extends the life of your waterproofing systems, prevents water damage, reduces long-term repair costs, and helps maintain the value and integrity of your property. Our maintenance programs can be customized to meet the specific needs of your facility.
