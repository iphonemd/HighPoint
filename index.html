Our Services

We provide comprehensive caulking and waterproofing solutions tailored to meet your specific needs and requirements.

Residential Caulking

We provide high-quality caulking services for homes, ensuring proper sealing of windows, doors, and other areas to prevent water infiltration.

Commercial Waterproofing

Our commercial waterproofing services protect your business properties from water damage, extending the life of your building structures.

Bathroom Sealing

We specialize in bathroom sealing to prevent water leakage and mold growth, ensuring a clean and safe environment in your bathrooms.

Exterior Waterproofing

Our exterior waterproofing services protect your property from environmental elements, preventing long-term structural damage.

Foundation Waterproofing

We provide comprehensive foundation waterproofing solutions to prevent water seepage and maintain the structural integrity of your property.

Maintenance Services

Our regular maintenance services ensure that your caulking and waterproofing systems remain effective and provide long-lasting protection.

About High Point

With over 15 years of experience in the industry, High Point has established itself as a leading provider of caulking and waterproofing services. Our team of skilled professionals is dedicated to delivering exceptional results on every project.

At High Point, we believe in building long-lasting relationships with our clients by providing reliable services and maintaining the highest standards of workmanship.
