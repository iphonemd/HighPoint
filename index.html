Silicone Roof Overlay Systems

Our silicone roof overlay systems provide a cost-effective alternative to full roof replacement, extending the life of your existing roof while improving energy efficiency and waterproofing performance. These seamless, monolithic membranes can be applied to virtually any existing roof substrate including TPO, PVC, EPDM, Build-up, Modified, and Metal roofing surfaces.

Compatible Roof Types:

  • TPO (Thermoplastic Polyolefin): Restore aging TPO membranes without the cost of removal
  • PVC: Extend the life of existing PVC roofs with superior UV protection
  • EPDM: Convert black EPDM roofs to reflective, energy-efficient surfaces
  • Build-up: Eliminate leaks and extend life on aging build-up roof systems
  • Modified Bitumen: Provide seamless protection over modified bitumen surfaces
  • Metal: Seal seams and stop rust on metal roofing systems

Benefits Include:

  • Significantly lower cost than roof replacement
  • Minimal disruption to building operations
  • Excellent UV resistance and weathering properties
  • High solar reflectivity for energy savings
  • Seamless, monolithic protection against leaks
  • Renewable and sustainable with future recoats
  • Warranty options up to 20 years

Applications:

Our silicone roof overlay systems are ideal for commercial, industrial, and institutional buildings with aging roofs that need restoration rather than replacement. The system can be applied to roofs with remaining service life that need improved waterproofing and energy efficiency.

Parking Garage Restoration

Our parking garage restoration services address the unique challenges faced by these critical structures, which are constantly exposed to traffic, weather extremes, and corrosive elements. We offer comprehensive solutions to repair structural damage, prevent water infiltration, and extend the useful life of your parking facility.

Our Restoration Services Include:

  • Concrete Patching: Professional repair of spalled, cracked, or deteriorated concrete surfaces to restore structural integrity
  • Carbon Fiber Wrap: Advanced structural reinforcement using high-strength carbon fiber materials to increase load capacity without adding significant weight
  • Protective Coatings: Application of specialized traffic-bearing membranes that provide waterproofing and skid resistance for driving and walking surfaces
  • Epoxy Injections: Precision repair of structural cracks using high-strength epoxy to restore concrete integrity and prevent water infiltration

Benefits Include:

  • Extended service life of your parking structure
  • Prevention of further deterioration and costly structural damage
  • Improved aesthetics and user experience
  • Enhanced safety for vehicles and pedestrians
  • Minimized disruption to facility operations during repairs
  • Comprehensive solutions from a single, experienced contractor

Applications:

Our parking garage restoration services are ideal for commercial, municipal and institutional parking structures experiencing concrete deterioration, water infiltration, surface wear, or structural concerns. We work with facility managers, property owners, and engineers to develop customized solutions that address your specific needs and budget constraints.

Traffic Coating

Our traffic coating systems provide superior protection for parking structures, garages, walkways, and other high-traffic areas exposed to vehicular and pedestrian traffic. These specialized coatings create a durable, waterproof membrane that protects your concrete surfaces from deterioration caused by water penetration, de-icing salts, UV exposure, and general wear and tear.

Benefits Include:

  • Extended service life of parking structures and decks
  • Protection against water intrusion and concrete deterioration
  • Resistance to oil, gas, and chemical spills
  • Improved skid resistance for safety
  • Customizable appearance with color options
  • Reduced maintenance costs and downtime

Applications:

Our traffic coating services are ideal for parking garages, stadium concourses, mechanical rooms, plaza decks, balconies, and any concrete surface exposed to vehicular or pedestrian traffic. We offer multiple coating options with varying thicknesses and durability levels to meet your specific needs and budget.
